Challenge and improve your geography knowledge in a fun education app

  • Test your knowledge by playing through seven unique challenges, from flags to locations on the map
  • Progress through the levels as the questions get harder
  • Learn at your own pace using the learn section of the app, featuring capitals cities, flags and countries and locations on the world map
  • View progress and try to beat your best scores

Challenges:

  • Capital Cities
  • World Flags
  • Maps: Countries
  • Maps: Capitals
  • Maps: Cities
  • Maps: Landmarks
  • Maps: Natural Features
