A must have companion app for anyone who enjoys hill walking in the United Kingdom or Ireland.

Track your progress in MyPeaks. Discover over 20,000 of the UK and Ireland's most spectacular hills and mountains. Create a list of targets to climb, check them off as you go, and track your progress as you get closer to reaching your hill climbing goals. Perfect for hill bagging.

  • Discover hills by recognised hill list (e.g. Munros and Wainwrights) or region
  • View a map containing all recognised hills and mountains in the UK and Ireland
  • View hill details including location details, grid reference and height
  • Mark hills as targets to climb
  • Add log entries to keep track of your progress
  • View progress breakdowns for each hill list and region

Includes over 20,000 hills, including the Munros, Corbetts, Grahams, Donalds, Hewitts, Nuttals, Wainwrights and Marilyns, plus many more.

Please note that not all of the hills and mountains included are accessible via public right of way, please check before attempting any climbs and seek landowners permission where necessary.
