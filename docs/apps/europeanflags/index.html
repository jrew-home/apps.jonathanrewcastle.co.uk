European Flags Challenge

How well do you know your European Flags?

How many European countries can you identify from their flag? Test your knowledge in this free, fun and easy to use quiz. Try to identify as many countries as you can, and improve your flag recognition. Choose from three difficulties to get started. Try to beat your scores as you begin to recognise more and more!

Features:

  • Identify which country a flag belongs to
  • Choice of three difficulties or a mixture of each
  • Timed and untimed game modes
  • Bonus points for several correct answers in a row
  • Share your score with your friends on Facebook or Twitter
  • View highscores and try to beat your best
