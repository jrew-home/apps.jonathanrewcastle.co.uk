Country Shapes

How well do you know your country shapes?

How many countries can you identify from their outline? Test your knowledge in this fun and easy to use quiz. Try to identify as many countries as you can, and improve your geographical knowledge. Choose from three difficulties to get started. Try to beat your scores as you begin to recognise more and more!

The quiz also includes a learning mode which can be used to look up the outline of any country, so you can learn at your own pace.

Features:

  • Identify which country is shown by the outline
  • Choice of three difficulties or a mixture of each
  • Timed and untimed game modes
  • Bonus points for several correct answers in a row
  • Share your score with your friends on Facebook or Twitter
  • View highscores and try to beat your best
  • Learn at your own pace using the country shapes reference
