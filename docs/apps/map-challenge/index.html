Explore the World

How many locations can you identify on the world map? Test your knowledge in this fun location-based quiz and learning tool, learning geography along the way.

Locations included:

  • Capitals: capital cities of the world (200 locations)
  • Cities: cities towns and built-up places (200 locations)
  • Landmarks: monuments, bridges, castles and other landmarks (200 locations)
  • Natural features: mountains, lakes, waterfalls and volcanoes (200 locations)

Each pack is divided into twenty levels of increasing difficulty, starting with the most well known locations in the world, and finishing with some less well known ones.

Track your scores and see how it improves as you recognise more and more locations.
