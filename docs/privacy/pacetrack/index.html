PaceTrack Pace Calculator Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application PaceTrack Pace Calculator ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by J-Rew Apps.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

This Application does not collect any User Provided Information.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, details of any errors encountered, and information about the way you use the Application, in order to help improve the Application. This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Yes. We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.
